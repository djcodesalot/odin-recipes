Best Daggum Deviled Eggs

Description

Quite possibly the best daggum deviled eggs you will ever put in your face AND you don't have to read my life story to get to it.

Ingredients

  • 12 Large hard-boiled eggs, peeled and sliced in half
  • 1/2 cup Mayonnaise
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 1/2 tsp Apple cider vinegar or pickle juice
  • 1/3 tsp Fine sea salt, or to taste
  • 1/8 tsp Fresh craed pepper, or to taste
  • 1/4 tsp Paprika, plus more for garnish
  • Chopped chives and parsley, for garnish

Steps

  1. Hardboil your eggs
  2. Once cooled, slice the eggs in half lengthwise
  3. Scoop out the egg yolk and place it in a large bowl
  4. Place the remaining egg whites on a large serving platter
  5. Use a fork to mash the yolks
  6. Add the mayo, mustard, vinegar, salt and pepper, and paprikaz
  7. Use a spoon or piping bag to add the yolk mix into the egg white halves
  8. Top with a sprinkle of parprika and garnish with chives and parsley
